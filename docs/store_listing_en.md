# Scorebook

## Short description

Fast, simple and offline scorekeeping for board and card games.

## Description

Scorebook replaces pen and paper when playing board and card games with friends. Pick players, enter each round and see the result instantly.

- Built-in and custom game templates
- Profile and friend photos
- Fast round entry and undo
- Automatic ranking, wins and game statistics
- Active games and history
- Local backups and file-based device transfer
- Light/dark theme, reduced motion, Turkish and English
- No account, ads or tracking; your data stays on your device

No internet connection required.
